Garlic Parmesan Roasted Chickpeas & Edamame

This savory and crispy snack combines chickpeas and edamame with garlic and Parmesan for a healthy and delicious treat.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup (150 g) edamame, shelled
  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) olive oil
  • 3 tbsp (24 g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp (5 g) garlic powder
  • 1 tsp (5 g) onion powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, edamame, olive oil, Parmesan, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread the mixture on a baking sheet in a single layer.
  4. Roast for 25-30 minutes, stirring halfway through, until crispy and golden brown.
  5. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
